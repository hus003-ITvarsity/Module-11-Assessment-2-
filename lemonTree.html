Lemon Tree

Rating: ★★★★☆

"The menu offers a variety of dishes, each with its own unique flavor profile."

Pros: Fresh and flavorful dishes, pleasant ambiance, and attentive service..

Cons: Some dishes can be a bit heavy, especially for larger appetites.

Lemon Tree offers a delightful dining experience, blending Mediterranean flavors with a modern twist. The bright, airy ambiance, coupled with the fresh, citrusy scents, creates a welcoming atmosphere.

Harrira Soup ★★★★☆: This hearty soup was full of flavor, with a blend of spices and vegetables. It was the perfect starter to a Moroccan meal.

Tagine with Lamb and Prunes ★★★★☆: The lamb was tender and flavorful, slow-cooked to perfection with the sweet and tangy prunes. The spices were balanced, creating a rich and complex dish.

Couscous with Vegetables ★★★★☆: The couscous was fluffy and well-seasoned, and the vegetables were cooked to perfection. The dish was light and refreshing, perfect for a warm day.

Basbousa ★★★★☆: This semolina cake was moist and flavorful, with a sweet syrup and a hint of orange blossom water. It was the perfect ending to a delicious meal.

Final Thoughts: Lemon Tree is a great choice for a refreshing and delicious meal. The menu offers a variety of dishes, each with its own unique flavor profile. While some dishes may be a bit heavy, the overall dining experience is enjoyable.
