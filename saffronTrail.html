Saffron Trail

Rating: ★★☆☆☆

"The reality fell short of expectations."

Pros:The ambiance, while generic, is somewhat pleasant.

Cons: Inconsistent food quality, slow service, and high prices.

Saffron Trail promised a flavorful journey through the vibrant markets of the Middle East. However, the reality fell short of expectations. The decor, while attempting to capture the essence of Middle Eastern culture, felt generic and uninspired.

Hummus and Pita Bread ★★☆☆☆: The hummus was bland and lacked the depth of flavor that characterizes a good hummus. The pita bread was dry and crumbly.

Mixed Grill ★★★☆☆: The meats were overcooked and dry. The accompanying sauces were bland and did little to enhance the flavor of the meat.

Baklava ★★☆☆☆: The pastry was overly sweet and the filling was dry. The nuts were sparse and lacked the crunch that makes baklava so enjoyable.

Final Thoughts: Saffron Trail is a missed opportunity. With a bit more attention to detail in the kitchen and a more attentive service staff, it could elevate its culinary offerings. However, based on our experience, we cannot recommend this restaurant.
